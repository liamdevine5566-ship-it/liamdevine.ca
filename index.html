BEHIND THE CAMERA

I'm a London, Ontario photographer with a background in landscape, wildlife, and motorsports — disciplines that trained me to read light fast and stay ready for the decisive moment.

That instinct runs through everything I shoot commercially: headshots, family sessions, and pets. I shoot primarily on Nikon Z8 and Fujifilm, with a home studio available for controlled sessions.
