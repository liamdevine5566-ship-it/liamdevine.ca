About

Behind the
camera

I'm a London-based photographer with a background in landscape, wildlife, and sports photography — disciplines — including motorsports — that trained me to read light fast and be ready for the unplanned moment.

That instinct now runs through everything I shoot: headshots, family sessions, pets, and sporting events. Every subject gets the same attention I'd give a bird in flight — preparation, patience, and a quick shutter when it counts.

I shoot primarily on Nikon Z8 and Fujifilm, with a home studio available for controlled sessions and the whole of London for everything else.
